🚀 Future Enhancements for DELI-cious

Here are some exciting ideas we plan to add to make the DELI-cious experience even better.

💳 Payment Integration

Currently, the app simulates order placement only. Future versions could support real-time payments using credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, or Google Pay.

📦 Delivery & Dine-In Options

Customers will be able to select whether they want their order delivered to their home or prefer to dine inside the DELI-cious restaurant.

📱 Mobile-Friendly Version

A mobile application for iOS and Android could make sandwich ordering more accessible and convenient from anywhere.

🌐 Online Ordering Portal

A web-based portal where customers can place orders remotely, view past orders, and even track delivery progress in real-time.

🎯 Customer Loyalty Program

Reward frequent customers with points and discounts based on the number of purchases or money spent.
